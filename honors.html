Fellowship and Awards

Graduate College Travel Award, 2024.

One of the few university-level fellowships granted by the ASU Graduate College to fund competitively selected students for professional development conferences and events.

Graduate and Professional Student Association Travel Award, 2024.

The most competitive travel grants provided by the ASU Graduate and Professional Student Association to support selected PhD students in pursuing individual research and professional growth through related travels.

School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences Travel Award, 2024.

Offered by ASU School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences to support graduate students' travels for developing individual research and professional competency.

Block Summer Research Grant Award, 2022.

One of the few university fellowships granted by the ASU Graduate College to support competitively selected high-quality PhD students in conducting cutting-edge research projects.

Dennis Young Graduate and Early Scholar Statistics Award, 2022.

Offered by ASU College of Liberal Arts and Sciences and endowed by Doctor Dennis Young in support of young scholars with outstanding academic records and strong research potential in Statistics

Dean's List of Outstanding Scholars, 2019 - 2020.

The highest level of undergraduate academic achievement at ASU that honors students with remarkable GPA performance and distinguishing academic excellence.

New American University Scholarship, 2019 - 2020.

One of the few ASU university-level merit-based awards provided to incoming international undergraduate students who demonstrate notable academic performance.
