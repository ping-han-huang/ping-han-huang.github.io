Hi, I'm Ping-Han

A PhD student at Arizona State University

I am a PhD student in Statistics at ASU School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences, with a concentration in machine learning (ML) and design of experiments (DOE). My current research focuses on two folds of the application. One is to improve the prediction performance of ML models through careful planning at the design stage, and another is to facilitate identifying optimal designs through ML algorithms such as reinforcement learning. I am looking forward to expanding my horizons with various creative research projects.
